Report a Problem

If a feature misbehaves on your school's EduPage, generate a diagnostic report so the issue can be fixed without access to your account. Open the affected EduPage page in another tab first, then generate the report.

When enabled, the report includes page structure (tags, classes, layout) but no grades, names, marks, or other text values.
